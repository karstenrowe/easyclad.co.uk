What is DIY hygienic wall cladding?

DIY Hygienic PVC Wall Cladding is the best alternative to ceramic tiles in areas such as commercial kitchens, showers, store rooms, laboratories and any other areas that requires a hygienic environment.

It provides a sealed finish that requires minimal maintenance and cleaning.

Product guide

Easyclad DIY cladding ranges provide a competitive option for the DIY enthusiast, fit out company or in house engineering teams that want to make savings on PVC sheets, trims and adhesives whilst having the benefit of fitting within their own time scales. Easyclad had supplied the construction industry for over 15 years.

Our team is experienced in advising customers on the appropriate materials and quantities that a job will require. We can also assist you with any fitting enquiries that you may have.

5 simple steps

  1. Measure the length and width of your walls.
  2. Measure the height of your walls.
  3. Count the internal and external corners.
  4. Have a good look at your walls - flat or uneven? Dry or damp? Tiled or painter? Don't worry about the condition. We will advise on the best solution.
  5. Place an order. Next day UK delivery.
