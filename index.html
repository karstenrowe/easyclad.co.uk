What is DIY hygienic wall cladding?

DIY Hygienic PVC Wall Cladding is the best alternative to ceramic tiles in areas such as commercial kitchens, showers, store rooms, laboratories and any other areas that requires a hygienic environment.

It provides a sealed finish that requires minimal maintenance and cleaning.

Single skin PVC wall cladding

Advanced PVC is a very versatile system which can be easily installed in almost any building.

K13/160 sheet linings and panelling.

Satin White Hygienic PVC Wall Cladding to EU Foodsafe standards. Fitted using a silicone free gasket system known as a Division Bar but can also be welded. 100% Virgin PVC. Full range of matching accessories available.

Product fire rating: B5 476-7 : 1971 : Class 1/0 (when bonded to a substrate). Class 1 fire rating but Class 'O' Fire Rating when adhered onto a non combustible substrate such as plaster, render, plasterboard, existing ceramic tiles and blockwork using our range of adhesives.

Typical applications

Food manufacturing areas, commercial kitchens, pharmaceutical areas, laboratories, showers, W.C.’s and wet hygienic areas.
