5 simple steps to order

  1. Measure the length and width of your walls.
  2. Measure the height of your walls.
  3. Count the internal and external corners.
  4. Have a good look at your walls - flat or uneven? Dry or damp? Tiled or painter? Don't worry about the condition. We will advise on the best solution.
  5. Place an order. Next day UK delivery.
